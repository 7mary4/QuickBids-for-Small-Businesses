The DUNS Number

The The DUNS number is a unique identification number for your businesses location. This unique id is used within the government registration process and is cross-referenced within several forms.

Do You Have a The DUNS Number?

Obtain a The DUNS Number

The registration form for obtaining this number is managed by Duns and Bradstreet. You will need to search for your business to see if it is in their system. You should receive a DUNS number within 1-2 business days after completing the online registration: Company Lookup and Registration
